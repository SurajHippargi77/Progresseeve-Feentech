Progresseeve Feentech – Company Profile

Progresseeve Feentech was established with the aim of delivering quality services in para-legal, contractual collection/recovery/resolution, and other related activities for both secured and unsecured loan collections. Headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra, the company focuses on the effective management and timely resolution of bad debts, non-performing assets, and asset reconstruction for financial institutions, companies, and banks.

Vision

To become a Pan-India Asset Restructuring and Resolution Company, committed to implementing best practices in debt recovery and client servicing.

Core Expertise

  • Research and analysis of client debtors
  • Case-specific strategic action plans backed by proprietary “know-how”
  • Documentation and out-of-court debt recovery processes
  • Legal recovery via in-court representation
